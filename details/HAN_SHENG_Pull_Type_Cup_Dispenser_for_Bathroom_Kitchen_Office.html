HAN SHENG Pull Type Cup Dispenser

This sleek and efficient cup dispenser is designed to bring order to your space, whether it's in the bathroom, kitchen, or office. Its innovative features make it a smart choice for anyone looking to keep paper cups organized and hygienic.

Constructed from durable ABS and PET materials, the HAN SHENG dispenser is built to last while remaining lightweight and easy to install. The flip cap design ensures each cup stays clean and protected from dust, making it perfect for high-traffic areas. A transparent window lets you easily check when cups need to be refilled, so you're always prepared and never left with a surprise shortage.

The self-adjusting steel claws securely grip cups of different sizes, ensuring they are held firmly in place. This design also means only one cup is accessible at a time, reducing waste and keeping your space looking tidy. With its wall mount design, the dispenser saves counter space and enhances overall organization, making it an essential addition to your home or workplace.
